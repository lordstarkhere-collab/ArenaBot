## Contribute to the wiki

Mech Arena Wiki is a collaborative wiki resource that is open for anyone to edit. You don't need special permission beyond [registering](https://mecharena.wiki.gg/wiki/Special:UserLogin/signup) an account to edit most pages, and your contributions can grow the wiki and help other players.

**Adding content**
:   - If you are unsure of what to do or how to create a page, search for a few articles on the same topic and see what they look like. You can always view the source code in a wiki and learn from what others have done.
    - An edit doesn't have to be massive; if you feel you don't want to create whole articles, then just fixing spelling errors and broken links is enough.
    - [Register](https://mecharena.wiki.gg/wiki/Special:UserLogin/signup) to edit and track your contributions.
