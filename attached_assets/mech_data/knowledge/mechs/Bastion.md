# Bastion

Bastion is a Legendary mech known for its defensive capabilities. As a Tier 8 mech, it specializes in absorbing damage and providing protection with its Blast Shield ability, making it a strong frontline presence on the battlefield.

## Ability: Blast Shield

*Bastion's Blast Shield ability provides protection while also delivering an EMP blast when depleted, making it ideal for defensive strategies.*

## Strategy

*Bastion is best used to lead charges or hold key positions. The Blast Shield ensures it can withstand significant damage while disrupting enemy Mechs upon breaking.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Use the shield to absorb incoming fire while positioning strategically for maximum EMP impact.*

### Countering

*Focus fire to break Bastion's shield quickly and be ready to evade the EMP explosion.*

### Builds

*Pair with defensive implants and weapons that allow for sustained engagements.*
