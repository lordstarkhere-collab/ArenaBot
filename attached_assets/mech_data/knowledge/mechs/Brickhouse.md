# Brickhouse

Brickhouse is a Legendary mech known for its Overcharger ability, amplifying both its own and nearby allies' weapon damage. As a Tier 8 support mech, Brickhouse excels in boosting team firepower, making it a valuable asset in coordinated team fights.

## Ability: Overcharger

*Brickhouse's Overcharger ability is essential for increasing damage output for both itself and nearby teammates, enhancing team synergy and effectiveness.*

## Strategy

*Position Brickhouse near allies who benefit from increased damage output, such as burst damage dealers or heavy weapons users.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Utilize Overcharger in team fights to maximize damage and turn engagements in your favor.*

### Countering

*Focus fire on Brickhouse early to prevent it from buffing its team’s damage output.*

### Builds

*Equip Brickhouse with weapons that benefit from its own Overcharger ability, such as high-DPS or burst weapons.*
