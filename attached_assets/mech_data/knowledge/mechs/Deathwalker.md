# Deathwalker

Deathwalker is a Legendary Tank mech known for its Reanimate ability. As a Tier 8 mech, it specializes in survivability and strategic repositioning, allowing it to return to a previously marked location upon death, regaining a portion of its HP. This makes Deathwalker a resilient and versatile option on the battlefield.

## Ability: Reanimate

*Deathwalker’s Reanimate ability allows it to return to a previous position upon death, regaining health and briefly becoming invulnerable.*

## Strategy

*Utilize Deathwalker's ability strategically to retreat from fights, bait enemies, and control engagements. Best used in conjunction with healing mechs or defensive setups.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Trigger Reanimate before entering combat to ensure survival and repositioning options in case of death.*

### Countering

*Focus on burst damage and tracking Deathwalker's previous location to anticipate its respawn point.*

### Builds

*Pair with support mechs or defensive weapons to enhance survivability and battlefield control.*
