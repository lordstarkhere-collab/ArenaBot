# Blockhorn

Blockhorn is a Legendary mech known for its ability to deploy an impassable Barricade that enhances allied damage within its Amp Zone. As a Tier 8 tank, Blockhorn excels at area denial and frontline support, making it an essential pick for strategic defense.

## Ability: Barricade

*Blockhorn's Barricade ability creates an impassable defensive structure while enhancing the offensive power of allies behind it.*

## Strategy

*Position Blockhorn strategically to control choke points and protect allies while they attack from behind its Barricade.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Utilize the Barricade to fortify positions and force enemies into disadvantageous fights.*

### Countering

*Flank around or use explosive weapons to bypass the Barricade’s protection.*

### Builds

*Equip Blockhorn with weapons that complement its tank role, such as Plasma Cannons or Arc Turrets.*
