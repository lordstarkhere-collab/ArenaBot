Salvor is a Legendary support mech specializing in healing allies with its unique Regen Shot ability. As a Tier 8 support mech, Salvor plays a crucial role in keeping teammates alive while maintaining battlefield presence.

## Ability: Regen Shot

*Salvor’s Regen Shot ability allows it to heal allies efficiently while maintaining its own survivability.*

## Strategy

*Position Salvor strategically to heal teammates while avoiding enemy fire. Work closely with front-line mechs to maximize team sustainability.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Use Regen Shot proactively to keep allies alive in critical fights. Prioritize healing high-impact teammates such as tanks or damage dealers.*

### Countering

*Disrupt Salvor's positioning to limit its ability to heal. Focus fire to eliminate it early in engagements.*

### Builds

*Equip Salvor with weapons that complement its support role, such as rapid-fire weapons to pressure enemies while healing.*
