# M.D.

The M.D. is an uncommon support mech known for its healing capabilities. Equipped with abilities that restore health to teammates and itself, M.D. is vital for maintaining team sustainability during intense battles. Its Repair Field ability ensures high healing speed and a large repair radius.

## Ability: Repair Field

The Repair Field creates a healing zone around M.D., restoring health to itself and allies within the radius. This makes it invaluable for sustained team efforts and holding critical positions.
