# MD

The MD is a Support mech known for its repair capabilities, specializing in sustaining teammates in battle. It features the ability to deploy a Repair Field, which restores health to all friendly mechs within its radius.

## Ability: Repair Field

*MD's Repair Field ensures prolonged team survivability, making it a valuable asset in extended battles.*

## Strategy

*Position MD to support teammates effectively, ensuring that the Repair Field reaches multiple allies during critical engagements.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Keep MD in proximity to damaged teammates and maintain a safe distance from enemies.*

### Countering

*Focus MD with high burst damage weapons to prevent extended healing from the Repair Field.*

### Builds

*Equip MD with weapons that allow it to provide additional support, such as Longarms for long-range harassment.*
