# Gatecrasher

Gatecrasher is a Legendary mech renowned for its Ion Pod ability, which offers both long-range damage potential and tactical area control. As a Tier 8 mech, Gatecrasher is designed for players who seek a combination of versatility and high power in their lineup.

## Ability: Ion Pod

*Gatecrasher’s Ion Pod enables players to tactically control areas by dealing sustained damage over time and disrupting enemy positioning.*

## Strategy

*Position Gatecrasher to utilize the Ion Pod for both direct damage and area denial. Target choke points or crowded enemy areas for maximum efficiency.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Deploy the Ion Pod to disrupt enemy formations and force them out of cover.*

### Countering

*Avoid clustering together and focus on destroying the Ion Pod to mitigate its damage potential.*

### Builds

*Equip Gatecrasher with high-damage weapons such as Disc Launchers or Longarm Rifles to complement its long-range capabilities.*
