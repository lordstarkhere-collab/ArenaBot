# Tengu

The Tengu is a rare scout mech capable of disrupting enemy formations with its powerful Sky Strike ability. This versatile mech can become untargetable while airborne and deliver devastating area damage upon landing, applying a stasis effect to enemies within range.

## Ability: Sky Strike

Sky Strike launches the Tengu into the air, making it untargetable and immune to EMP effects. Upon landing, it deals significant damage to enemies within a radius and applies a Stasis Effect, immobilizing affected enemies temporarily.
