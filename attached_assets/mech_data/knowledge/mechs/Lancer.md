# Lancer

The Lancer is a scout mech known for its dexterity and maneuverability, specializing in capturing control points and spotting enemies. It features Jump Jets for repositioning or avoiding enemy fire.

## Ability: Jump Jets

*When Jump Jets is activated the mech boosts into the air, ascending most medium sized obstacles.*

## Strategy

*Medium obstacles can be scaled from to reach even higher obstacles. Low hp and energy plagues this mech in the higher levels. But with the unique vantage point and premium cover, this mech can bring a lot to the table with appropriate practice and map knowledge*

### Countering

*Lancer is extremely vulnerable on the ground, eliminating it while it is attempting to jump or if it fails a jump is your best bet. In the event it has reached its elevated position, guided weaponary are your greatest tool, followed by other arcing trajectory weapons such as nade launcher and cryo nade launcher. Splash and sniper weapons may be utilized circumstantially.*

### Builds

*Because of the limited energy, low hp, low rarity, and early receipt, lancer is seldom used beyond the first few hundred battles.*
In this early stage, you often see lancer with javelin rack 4's, rpg's, and for the adventurous sg4's. Usually around this time the mech is phased out for bigger mechs with higher energy. If you wish to continue utilizing this mech, i would strongly suggest advancing to javelin rack combinations: 4 and 6, 6 and 6, 8 and 6, 8 and 8. If javelins is not a weapon available, i would next reccomned missle racks of the 6 and 8 energy variants. This can well carry you into midgame, and because of ths low cost of upgrading lancer, you will have the earliest chance to access mods through rank 6. Once you find these weapons under performing, you will most likely rotate to legendary weapons to deal with stronger opponents. The best for lancer are strike rocket and storm racks. But pods and other low facetime weapons are competent as well.
