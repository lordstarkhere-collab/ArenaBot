# Outlaw

Outlaw is a Legendary mech featuring the powerful Foul Play ability. As a Tier 9 mech, it excels at disabling enemy capabilities while dealing area damage, making it a key strategic option in competitive play.

## Ability: Foul Play

*Outlaw's Foul Play ability allows it to disable enemy Mechs' Weapons and Abilities, making it a valuable choice for disrupting key enemy strategies.*

## Strategy

*Position Outlaw to maximize the area-of-effect radius of Foul Play. Time the ability to disable priority targets and turn the tide of battle.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Use Foul Play strategically to target high-priority enemies, such as those with crowd control abilities or heavy firepower.*

### Countering

*Maintain distance and avoid grouping to minimize the impact of Foul Play.*

### Builds

*Equip Outlaw with weapons like Longarms or Javelins to complement its disruption abilities.*
