# Solis

Solis is a Legendary support mech equipped with the Optic Override ability, which blinds enemies and disables their ability to target effectively. As a Tier 8 mech, Solis is a strategic asset in battle, disrupting enemy vision and rendering them vulnerable to follow-up attacks.

## Ability: Optic Override

*Solis's Optic Override blinds enemies in its radius, preventing them from effectively using weapons or abilities.*

## Strategy

*Use Solis to disrupt enemy formations by blinding high-priority targets, giving your team a tactical advantage.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Position Solis strategically to maximize the impact of Optic Override, ensuring that enemy damage dealers are affected.*

### Countering

*Keep a safe distance from Solis or use abilities that grant immunity to vision impairments.*

### Builds

*Pair Solis with long-range weapons to capitalize on disabled enemies, or equip it with close-range weapons to eliminate blinded foes.*
