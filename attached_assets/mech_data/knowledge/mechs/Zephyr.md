## Overview

Zephyr is an Epic Support Mech designed for battlefield control and disruption. Its Shock Pulse ability unleashes a disabling EMP wave, rendering enemy Mechs immobile and unable to attack for a short duration. Zephyr is an invaluable asset for turning the tide in team fights and countering aggressive enemies.

## Ability: Shock Pulse

The \*\*Shock Pulse\*\* ability emits a powerful EMP wave that temporarily disables nearby enemy Mechs. The ability lasts for 3 seconds, with a radius of 30 meters, making it an excellent tool for halting enemy advances or disrupting enemy formation during critical engagements.
