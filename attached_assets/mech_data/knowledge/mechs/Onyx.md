# Onyx

Onyx is a Legendary mech known for its powerful Quad Rocket ability. As a Tier 8 mech, it specializes in high burst damage, launching rockets in a straight line that deal significant splash damage upon impact. Onyx is a popular choice for players who value heavy firepower and area control.

## Ability: Quad Rocket

*Onyx's Quad Rocket ability is its signature weapon, allowing it to dominate battles with devastating area damage.*

## Strategy

*Position Onyx to target clusters of enemies and maximize the splash damage from Quad Rocket. Pair with allies who can control enemy movements to ensure rockets hit their mark.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Utilize Onyx's Quad Rocket ability to disrupt enemy formations and secure kills on tightly packed opponents.*

### Countering

*Avoid grouping up to minimize the effectiveness of Onyx's Quad Rocket splash damage.*

### Builds

*Equip Onyx with high-damage weapons like Missile Racks or Pulse Cannons to complement its burst damage ability.*
