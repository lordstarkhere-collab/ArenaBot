# Aegis

Aegis is a robust Epic-tier tank mech designed to dominate the battlefield with its advanced defensive capabilities. Equipped with the Dome Shield ability, Aegis excels at protecting itself and its allies by deploying a durable and expansive shield. Its high HP, steady speed, and unique implant compatibility make it a formidable presence in any match.
