## Overview

*Dreadnought is a powerful legendary attacker mech designed to dominate the battlefield with its high energy capacity and devastating Wipe Out ability. It excels in taking down enemies with missiles and inflicting EMP effects, offering both raw damage and tactical utility. When Wipe Out kills an enemy, the ability cooldown resets, providing momentum in aggressive scenarios.*

## Ability: Wipe Out

*Wipe Out unleashes a barrage of missiles that devastates a wide area, dealing significant damage to enemies and inflicting an EMP effect. When this ability eliminates an enemy, Dreadnought gains an instant Speed boost, and its Ability Cooldown is reset, making it an excellent choice for aggressive players.*
