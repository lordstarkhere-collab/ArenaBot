# Panther

Panther is an Epic mech known for its strategic capabilities on the battlefield. Equipped with the unique "Stasis Barrier" ability, it can block bullets and apply a slowing stasis effect to enemies that enter the barrier. Panther excels as an Attacker class mech, combining control and offensive power.

## Overview

Panther is a tactical mech designed to control the flow of battle. Its Stasis Barrier provides utility by both blocking projectiles and hindering enemy movements, making it a versatile choice for strategic players.
