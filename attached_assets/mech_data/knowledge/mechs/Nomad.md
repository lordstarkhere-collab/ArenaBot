# Nomad

Nomad is a Legendary scout mech that combines speed, agility, and high damage potential. As a Tier 8 mech, it features the powerful Chain Dash ability, allowing it to dash between enemies, deal significant damage, and reduce incoming damage during the dash.

## Ability: Chain Dash

*Chain Dash allows Nomad to rapidly move between enemies, dealing significant damage and temporarily reducing incoming damage, enabling it to dominate in chaotic skirmishes.*

## Strategy

*Leverage Chain Dash to hit multiple enemies in succession. Focus on isolated or vulnerable targets to maximize its damage potential.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Nomad is ideal for hit-and-run tactics and disrupting enemy formations. Its high mobility makes it perfect for capturing points and breaking through defenses.*

### Countering

*Anticipate Nomad's dashes and maintain distance. Use crowd control or area-of-effect weapons to limit its effectiveness.*

### Builds

*Pair Nomad with mid-range burst weapons like Missile Racks or Carbines to amplify its damage output and complement its mobility.*
