# Seeker

Seeker is a Legendary mech that uses its Warp ability to teleport directly to enemies, dealing significant damage and activating a personal shield. This Tier 9 mech is designed for precision strikes, making it a favorite for tactical players.

## Ability: Warp

*Seeker's Warp ability allows it to teleport to enemies and unleash devastating attacks. The shield activated upon arrival provides survivability in heated battles.*

## Strategy

*Use Seeker's Warp to target vulnerable enemies, such as snipers or support mechs. The personal shield ensures Seeker can sustain damage while completing its assault.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Master the Warp's timing to disrupt enemy formations and eliminate high-value targets.*

### Countering

*Avoid clustering together, as Seeker's Warp can turn the tide in team fights.*

### Builds

*Equip Seeker with weapons that enhance its mobility and capitalize on its ability to deal bonus damage.*
