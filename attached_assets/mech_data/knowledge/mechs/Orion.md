## Overview

Orion is a support mech specialized in amplifying team damage and disrupting enemies. Its unique ability, \*\*Hunter's Mark\*\*, boosts ally firepower by making marked targets take back-hit damage from all weapon attacks while slowing them down with a stasis effect.

## Ability: Hunter's Mark

Orion’s \*\*Hunter's Mark\*\* ability fires a tracking flare that applies a Marked Effect and stasis to an enemy mech. Marked mechs take amplified back-hit damage from all weapon attacks, making them vulnerable targets.

## Strategy

Orion thrives as a support mech in team compositions. By marking enemies, it ensures team firepower is maximized, while its stasis effect disrupts enemy mobility. Positioning is critical, as Orion must stay safe while marking high-priority targets.

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

- Focus on marking enemy DPS mechs to maximize team advantage.
- Stay behind frontline mechs to avoid direct damage.

### Countering

- Utilize mechs with immunity to stasis effects to counter Hunter's Mark.
- Engage Orion with stealth or high-damage burst mechs to prevent it from supporting allies effectively.

### Builds

- Pair with long-range weapons to mark targets from a safe distance.
- Equip cooldown reduction implants to ensure frequent use of Hunter's Mark.
