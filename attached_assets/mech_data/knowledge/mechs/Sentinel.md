# Sentinel

The Sentinel is a Tank mech designed to absorb damage, protect allies, and attract attention on the battlefield. It features the ability to deploy a Tall Shield, a vertical barrier capable of blocking arcing projectiles. The shield regenerates faster as the Sentinel takes damage at higher levels.

## Ability: Tall Shield

*Sentinel's Tall Shield is highly effective at blocking arcing projectiles, making it ideal for tanking against high-damage weapons.*

## Strategy

*Sentinel excels at soaking damage and drawing attention. Use the Tall Shield strategically to protect allies and hold chokepoints.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Position Sentinel at key locations to block enemy projectiles and protect your teammates.*

### Countering

*Flank Sentinel or use weapons that bypass shields to counter its defensive capabilities.*

### Builds

*Equip Sentinel with weapons that complement its tanking role, such as Plasma Cannons or Missile Racks.*
