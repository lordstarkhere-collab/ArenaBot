Scorpius is a Legendary mech known for its advanced targeting and destructive capability. As a Tier 7 mech, it brings unique firepower to the battlefield with its Javelin Array ability, capable of devastating enemies without alerting them to its lock.

## Ability: Javelin Array

*The Javelin Array is a devastating built-in weapon designed to target and eliminate enemies with precision. Its ability to lock onto targets without notification makes it a deadly tool in battle.*

## Strategy

*Leverage the Javelin Array's stealth locking ability to eliminate high-value targets before they can react. Stay mobile and use cover to maximize survivability.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Scorpius excels in mid-to-long-range engagements. Pair it with weapons that complement its ability to deal burst damage.*

### Countering

*Keep moving unpredictably to make it harder for Scorpius to lock onto you. Use obstacles to block the Javelin Array's path.*

### Builds

*Pair Scorpius with Rocket Mortars or Javelin Racks to emphasize its mid-range dominance.*
