## Ability: Phase Cloak

*Phase Cloak allows Eclipse to evade detection and debuffs while gaining a significant boost in speed, making it a formidable scout on the battlefield.*

## Strategy

*Use Phase Cloak to infiltrate enemy lines, capture objectives, and escape dangerous situations. Leverage its speed to outmaneuver opponents.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Eclipse is ideal for high-speed objectives and reconnaissance missions. Its ability to remain undetected provides tactical advantages.*

### Countering

*Predict Eclipse's movements and use area-of-effect attacks to limit its effectiveness while cloaked.*

### Builds

*Equip Eclipse with high-mobility weapons such as Arc Torrets or Carbines to complement its stealth and speed.*
