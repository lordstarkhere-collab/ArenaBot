# Redeemer

The Redeemer is an Epic scout mech with the ability to rewind its position and remove debuffs using its Backtrack skill. Its high speed and ability to negate debuffs make it a versatile option for players who value strategic positioning and utility.

## Ability: Backtrack

The Backtrack ability allows Redeemer to return to its previous position, providing strategic repositioning opportunities and nullifying any active debuffs when upgraded. This makes it particularly effective for escaping dangerous situations and maintaining its operational capacity.
