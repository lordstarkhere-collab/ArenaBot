# Surge

Surge is a Legendary scout mech that excels in speed and disruptive abilities. As a Tier 7 mech, it uses its unique Storm Dash ability to cause havoc on the battlefield, combining an EMP Smash with stealth for a deadly and evasive strategy.

## Ability: Storm Dash

*Storm Dash enables Surge to move quickly across the battlefield, disrupting enemies with an EMP Smash and evading detection with its Stealth Buff.*

## Strategy

*Use Storm Dash to disable groups of enemies and escape dangerous situations. Pair this ability with quick reflexes to maximize its potential.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Surge thrives in fast-paced gameplay. Use it to capture objectives, disrupt enemy plans, or support allies in tight situations.*

### Countering

*Anticipate Surge's movements and maintain distance. Utilize area denial weapons to reduce its effectiveness.*

### Builds

*Combine Surge with light, fast-firing weapons such as Carbines or Pulsars to complement its mobility and disruptive capabilities.*
