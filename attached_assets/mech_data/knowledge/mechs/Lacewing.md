# Lacewing

Lacewing is a Legendary scout mech that excels in tactical disruption and area denial. As a Tier 8 mech, its Mine Jump ability allows it to leap into the air while deploying high-damage mines, making it a powerful choice for controlling the battlefield.

## Ability: Mine Jump

*Mine Jump allows Lacewing to disrupt enemy movements and deal high damage by deploying mines while jumping, creating opportunities to control the battlefield.*

## Strategy

*Utilize Mine Jump to ambush enemies or secure critical areas. Its mines provide effective area denial and are great for countering enemy advances.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Lacewing excels at controlling choke points and disorienting enemies. Its mines are ideal for defending objectives or cutting off escape routes.*

### Countering

*Maintain distance from Lacewing and avoid areas where mines are deployed. Use ranged attacks to neutralize it before it can deploy its mines.*

### Builds

*Pair Lacewing with explosive or area-of-effect weapons to enhance its battlefield control capabilities.*
