# Redox

Redox is a Legendary mech known for its corrosive abilities, specializing in applying status effects to disrupt enemies. As a Tier 7 mech, it excels in high-level battles with its Caustic Blast ability, which damages and slows enemies over time.

## Ability: Caustic Blast

*Redox's Caustic Blast is a powerful tool for area control, applying corrosion and slowing enemies to disrupt their movements and strategies.*

## Strategy

*Position Redox to maximize the effectiveness of Caustic Blast, targeting clustered enemies to deal massive area damage and apply status effects.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Use Redox in team compositions to capitalize on its ability to debuff enemies and create openings for allies.*

### Countering

*Maintain distance from Redox to avoid its Caustic Blast radius or use shields to mitigate its effects.*

### Builds

*Equip Redox with weapons that complement its debuffing role, such as Missile Racks for high burst damage or Plasma Cannons for sustained fire.*
