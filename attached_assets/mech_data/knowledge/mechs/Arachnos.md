# Arachnos

The Arachnos is a rare support mech equipped with a Spider Turret that can deal significant damage to nearby enemies. Its unique ability allows it to deploy indestructible turrets, providing both offensive capabilities and area control on the battlefield.

## Ability: Spider Turret

Spider Turret allows Arachnos to deploy a small, destructible turret that becomes active when enemies approach. The turret deals damage over time, making it effective for area control and supporting allies in combat.
