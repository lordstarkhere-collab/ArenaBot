# Mimicker

Mimicker is a Legendary scout mech that adapts dynamically to its enemies. With its Adaptation ability, it scans an enemy and gains temporary boosts tailored to the enemy's role, making it a versatile and unpredictable threat on the battlefield.

## Ability: Adaptation

*Mimicker’s Adaptation ability enables it to adjust to its target’s role, granting tailored boosts such as damage, shields, stealth, or repair capabilities. This makes it a versatile and adaptable mech in any situation.*

## Strategy

*Use Adaptation to gain an advantage against specific enemies. Mimicker shines when targeting enemies with critical roles, neutralizing them effectively.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Mimicker works best in a team where it can adapt to fill gaps in capability. Pair it with support mechs for a balanced approach or use it to counter high-priority targets.*

### Countering

*Maintain unpredictability by frequently changing your mech’s positioning and strategy. Avoid prolonged engagements to limit Mimicker’s ability to adapt effectively.*

### Builds

*Equip Mimicker with a versatile weapon loadout that complements its adaptability, such as weapons with both long-range and close-range potential.*
