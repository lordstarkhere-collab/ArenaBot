# Hemlock

Hemlock is a Legendary support mech specializing in enemy disruption through its ECM Shot ability. As a Tier 8 mech, it applies the Daze effect, disabling its target after a short delay. If the target is damaged during this effect, the Daze is replaced with a shorter EMP effect. Hemlock is a strong choice for players looking to control engagements and neutralize threats.

## Ability: ECM Shot

*Hemlock's ECM Shot is a tactical ability designed to disrupt enemy mechs, forcing them into a vulnerable state by disabling their systems.*

## Strategy

*Utilize Hemlock’s ECM Shot to prevent enemy mechs from using their abilities and control the battlefield effectively.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Target key enemy mechs like snipers or tanks to neutralize their impact on the battlefield.*

### Countering

*Avoid getting hit by ECM Shot or use mechs with status-clearing abilities to remove the debuff.*

### Builds

*Equip Hemlock with weapons that complement its disruption capabilities, such as Arc Turrets or Stasis Beam Rifles.*
