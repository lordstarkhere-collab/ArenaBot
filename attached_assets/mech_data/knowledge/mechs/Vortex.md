# Vortex

Vortex is a Legendary tank mech equipped with the Nanobot Field ability. As a Tier 8 mech, it specializes in both defensive and offensive support, creating an area of nanobots that damage enemies while reducing incoming damage for itself and teammates.

## Ability: Nanobot Field

*Vortex's Nanobot Field ability is a versatile tool, providing both offensive and defensive capabilities by damaging enemies while reducing incoming damage.*

## Strategy

*Vortex thrives in close-range fights, where it can make full use of its ability to weaken enemies while sustaining itself and teammates.*

## Guide/Tips

### Usage

*Stick to the frontline and use Nanobot Field to maximize sustain and damage.*

### Countering

*Maintain distance to avoid prolonged exposure to the Nanobot Field.*

### Builds

*Equip Vortex with weapons that complement its tank role, such as Arc Torrents or Plasma Cannons.*
