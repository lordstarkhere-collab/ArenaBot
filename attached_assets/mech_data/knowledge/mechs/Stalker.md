# Stalker

The Stalker is a rare attacker mech built for high-risk, high-reward playstyles. Its Predator Drive ability amplifies its offensive potential by increasing both the damage dealt and received, while providing healing for each enemy it eliminates. This makes the Stalker a formidable presence in aggressive combat scenarios.

## Ability: Predator Drive

Predator Drive activates the mech's core amplifiers, increasing the damage it deals and takes. Additionally, it recovers a percentage of its HP for each enemy eliminated, making it a perfect choice for aggressive players.
