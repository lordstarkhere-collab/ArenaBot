# Weapon DPS Calculation

_How DPS is computed_

> Regular incomes to be expected every week, may vary from one week to another
> Don't worry about Credits and Blueprints, those are relatively plentiful. The bottleneck that would dictate your progress is A-Coins.
> You should use your ressources only when requested by an event instead of spending them ASAP.
> New players looking to progress their hangar would be better served by not upgrading anything and instead using resources to buy new gear that unlocks the next tiers in Gear hub. Most low tier gear can't compete with higher tier so resources sunk into those upgrades might eventually be obsolete and wasted.
