| General - Weapons | If progressing towards end-game consider 8+12 energy weapons. They can be combined for 8,12,16,20,24 energy - full progression with only 2 weapons.   If progressing towards late game (more f2p friendly goal) consider 6+10 energy weapons (combined for 6,12,16,20 and cheaper than 8+12).  Either way upgrade the 12 or 10 weapon to rank 6, and there's no need to upgrade the 8 or 6 weapon beyond rank 5.  Specific weapon advice is WIP. there are a few things that might make others effective vs mods as we're learning about them. Top weapons (effective against mods): Storm Rack, Nades, Revokers, Minigun, Viper, Ember Gun Other viable weapons (vs. mods): Chain Gun, Disruptor, |  |
| Pilots | Don't upgrade rare (blue) pilots above rank 4 - save those pilot marks for epic or legendary pilots.  It's a good idea to maintain a minimum balance of 1 million credits in case of credit-based legendary pilot ECR  (see the bottom of the "Calendar Tracking" tab) - an excellent value you wouldn't want to miss. |  |
| Mods | This advice is WIP - still learning about this feature.  1. Don't install mods on temporary mechs, only on your best end-game mechs. It's okay to play without mods during most of your progression, save up the mod parts. 2. Don't install epic mods, ever. They are a waste of parts. Save up elite mod parts and only install legendary mods. 3. Perimeter ML shield is probably the most practical mod in terms of cost/benefit ratio. In the 3 weeks it'd take you to get the elite parts to buy it you'll also accumulate the basic parts to upgrade it. |  |
| Hangar slots | In the early game there is no rush to unlock all 5 hangar slots. You can participate in 5v5 tournament (not FFA tournament) and all battle modes with only 3 mechs. Don't unlock the 4th (or 5th) hangar slot before you have a strong build to populate that slot with. |  |
| General - Gear Hub Tiers | Don't invest into low tier mechs, low energy weapons, and high energy weapons that are not epic or legendary. At the first few tiers you can use the cheapest path (see "Cost:Purchases" tab). |  |
